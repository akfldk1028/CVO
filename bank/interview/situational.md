# Situational Interview Questions (상황 대처 질문)

## 기술적 상황
1. "Your digital twin dashboard shows incorrect sensor data. How would you debug this?"
2. "A client wants to add a new feature to the digital twin, but the deadline is tomorrow. What do you do?"
3. "The 3D model is too heavy and the application is lagging. How would you fix this?"
4. "You discover a security vulnerability in the IoT data pipeline. What steps do you take?"

## 팀/커뮤니케이션 상황
5. "Your manager asks you to use a technology you've never used before. How do you approach it?"
6. "A teammate disagrees with your technical approach. How do you handle it?"
7. "You need to explain digital twin technology to a client who has no technical background. How would you do it?"
8. "You realize you won't meet the sprint deadline. What do you do?"

## 의사결정 상황
9. "You have two possible solutions to a problem. One is quick but temporary, the other is slow but permanent. Which do you choose and why?"
10. "A client requests a feature that you think is technically unnecessary. How do you handle it?"
11. "You find a bug in production but the team is about to release a new feature. What do you prioritize?"
12. "Your team is split between using Unity and Unreal for a new project. How do you make the decision?"

## 답변 구조
```
1. 상황 인식: "First, I would assess the situation by..."
2. 계획 수립: "Then, I would plan to..."
3. 실행: "I would take action by..."
4. 소통: "I would communicate with my team by..."
5. 결과/교훈: "From this, I learned that..."
```

## 유용한 표현
- "My first step would be to..."
- "I would prioritize... because..."
- "I would communicate this to... by..."
- "In my experience, the best approach is..."
- "I would balance... with..."
