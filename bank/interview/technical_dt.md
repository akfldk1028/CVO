# Technical Interview Questions - Digital Twin (기술 면접)

## 디지털트윈 기본 개념
1. "What is a digital twin? Can you explain it simply?"
2. "What are the key components of a digital twin system?"
3. "What is the difference between a digital twin and a simulation?"
4. "How do you ensure data accuracy between the physical and digital twin?"

## 3D / 시각화
5. "How do you create 3D models for digital twins?"
6. "What is your experience with Unity/Unreal for digital twin visualization?"
7. "How do you optimize 3D rendering performance for large-scale models?"
8. "How do you handle Level of Detail (LOD) in digital twin applications?"
9. "What file formats do you typically work with for 3D assets?"

## IoT / 데이터 연동
10. "How do you connect IoT sensors to a digital twin?"
11. "Explain the data pipeline from sensor to visualization."
12. "What protocols do you use for real-time data? (MQTT, WebSocket, etc.)"
13. "How do you handle data latency in real-time digital twins?"
14. "How do you deal with sensor data that is noisy or incomplete?"

## 아키텍처 / 설계
15. "Describe the architecture of a digital twin system you built."
16. "How do you design APIs for digital twin data exchange?"
17. "What is your approach to scaling a digital twin platform?"
18. "How do you handle version control for 3D assets and configurations?"

## 기술 스택
19. "What tech stack do you prefer for digital twin development and why?"
20. "Compare Unity vs Unreal for digital twin applications."
21. "What databases do you use for time-series IoT data?"
22. "How do you use REST API vs MQTT in your projects?"

## 실전 / 응용
23. "Describe how you would build a digital twin for a smart factory."
24. "How do you implement predictive maintenance using digital twins?"
25. "What are the biggest challenges in digital twin development?"
26. "How do you test a digital twin system?"

## 답변 팁
- 구체적인 기술명 사용 (Unity, MQTT, InfluxDB 등)
- 숫자로 표현 ("reduced latency by 40%")
- 트레이드오프 설명 ("We chose X over Y because...")
