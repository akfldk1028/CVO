# Behavioral Interview Questions (행동 면접)

## STAR Method 가이드

| 단계 | 영어 | 설명 | 예시 시작 |
|------|------|------|----------|
| **S**ituation | 상황 | 언제, 어디서 | "In my previous role at..." |
| **T**ask | 과제 | 무엇을 해야 했나 | "I was responsible for..." |
| **A**ction | 행동 | 내가 뭘 했나 | "I decided to..." / "I implemented..." |
| **R**esult | 결과 | 어떻게 됐나 | "As a result..." / "This led to..." |

## 질문 뱅크

### 팀워크
1. "Tell me about a time you worked with a difficult team member."
2. "Describe a situation where you had to collaborate with a non-technical team."
3. "Give me an example of when you helped a teammate."

### 문제 해결
4. "Tell me about a challenging bug you solved."
5. "Describe a time when a project didn't go as planned."
6. "Tell me about a time you had to learn a new technology quickly."

### 리더십 / 주도성
7. "Tell me about a time you took initiative."
8. "Describe when you had to make a decision without your manager."
9. "Give me an example of when you improved a process."

### 실패 / 학습
10. "Tell me about a time you made a mistake at work."
11. "Describe a project that failed. What did you learn?"
12. "Tell me about negative feedback you received and how you handled it."

### 시간 관리 / 압박
13. "Tell me about a time you met a tight deadline."
14. "Describe how you prioritize when you have multiple tasks."
15. "Tell me about working under pressure."

## 유용한 연결 표현
- "First, I..." → "Then, I..." → "Finally, I..."
- "The main challenge was..."
- "What I learned from this was..."
- "If I could do it again, I would..."
- "This experience taught me..."
