# Self Introduction Questions (자기소개)

## 기본 자기소개
1. "Tell me about yourself."
2. "Walk me through your resume."
3. "What do you do currently?"

## 경력 관련
4. "How did you get into digital twin development?"
5. "What is your experience with real-time 3D applications?"
6. "Describe your role in your current/previous company."
7. "What projects are you most proud of?"

## 디지털트윈 경험 심화
8. "Can you describe a digital twin project you built from scratch?"
9. "What industries have you worked in with digital twin technology?"
10. "How do you typically start a new digital twin project?"

## 모범 답변 구조 (STAR 기반)

### 자기소개 템플릿:
```
1. 인사 + 이름
   "Hi, my name is [Name]."

2. 현재 역할 (1문장)
   "I'm a digital twin developer at [Company]."

3. 핵심 경험 (2-3문장)
   "I have [X] years of experience in [skills]."
   "I've worked on projects involving [specifics]."

4. 왜 이 포지션인지 (1-2문장)
   "I'm excited about this role because..."

5. 마무리
   "I'd love to contribute my experience to your team."
```

### 핵심 표현:
- "I specialize in..." (전문 분야)
- "I have hands-on experience with..." (실무 경험)
- "I'm passionate about..." (열정 있는 분야)
- "My background is in..." (배경)
