# Daily Conversation Topics (일상 대화 주제)

## 일상
1. What did you do today? (오늘 뭐 했어?)
2. What did you have for lunch? (점심 뭐 먹었어?)
3. How was your weekend? (주말 어땠어?)
4. What are your plans for this weekend? (이번 주말 계획?)
5. Do you have any hobbies? (취미 있어?)

## 직장 생활
6. How was work today? (오늘 일은 어땠어?)
7. What are you working on these days? (요즘 뭐 작업 중이야?)
8. Do you like working from home or office? (재택 vs 출근?)
9. What tools do you use at work? (업무에 어떤 도구 써?)
10. Tell me about your team. (팀에 대해 말해줘)

## 기술/개발
11. What programming language do you like most? (좋아하는 프로그래밍 언어?)
12. What's a recent tech news that interested you? (최근 관심 있는 기술 뉴스?)
13. Do you attend any meetups or conferences? (밋업이나 컨퍼런스 가?)
14. What do you want to learn next? (다음에 뭐 배우고 싶어?)
15. How did you start coding? (코딩 어떻게 시작했어?)

## 일상 + 영어
16. Do you watch any English content? (영어 콘텐츠 봐?)
17. What's your favorite way to study English? (영어 공부 어떻게 해?)
18. Have you traveled abroad? (해외 여행 가봤어?)
19. What's your morning routine? (아침 루틴이 뭐야?)
20. What kind of music do you listen to? (어떤 음악 들어?)

## 미래/목표
21. Where do you see yourself in 5 years? (5년 후 어디 있을 것 같아?)
22. What's your dream company? (꿈의 회사는?)
23. If you could work anywhere in the world, where? (세계 어디서든 일할 수 있다면?)
24. What skills do you want to improve? (어떤 스킬 향상시키고 싶어?)
25. What motivates you at work? (일할 때 동기부여가 뭐야?)
